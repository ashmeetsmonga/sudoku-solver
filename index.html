How To Play

To fill a cell, select the number from the bottom row and click on the cell

To change number in cell, select another number and click on the cell

To empty the cell, select 0 from the bottom row and click on the cell

To check whether the filled cells are correct or not, press the 'Check' button

Press 'Uncheck' button to decolor the cells

Press 'Solve' to solve the sudoku

Press 'Solve (Visualize)' to see the solving process.
